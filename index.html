Learn How Chit Funds Works!

Chit funds have been playing a stellar role even before the advent of modern banking era, in coming to rescue of the needy at affordable terms.

  • Cost of intermediation is the lowest.
  • Interest rate is determined by the subscribers themselves.
  • The money that you borrow is against your own future contributions.
  • Money can be readily drawn in an emergency or could be continued as an investment.

A chit fund is both a saving and borrowing option. If you don't bid for the money till the end, it is like a recurring deposit. You get your money back at the end of the chit's term
